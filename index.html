Zhaoming Xie

zxie47@cs.ubc.ca

I am a third year postgraduate at Fudan University, supervised by Professor Wei Li. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree in computer science at Michiel van de Panne. I completed my BEng in Computer Engineering at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology and MS in Electrical Engineering and Mathematics at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I also did internships with NVIDIA, Intel and Electronic Arts. My research focuses on reinforcement learning for robotics and physics-based character animation.
