I am a third-year postgraduate student at Fudan University, supervised by Prof. Wei Li. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree in computer science at Dalian University of Technology. In September, 2020, I was admitted to study for a master degree in Fudan University without entrance examination. I also did an internship at Netease Games from July, 2022 to September, 2022. My previous research work can be summarized as discovering high-quality and diverse solutions with reinforcement learning, generative model and evolutionary algorithms. Especially, I focus on simulated robotic control tasks.

Currently, I'm obsessed with AI-driven, physics-based character animation, which is an interdisciplinary research of computer graphics and deep learning. Since my enthusiasm to watch athletic sports, I desire simulated characters can perform atheletic skills like professional athletes. To that end, I wrote a brief research proposal Learn to Sprint 100 Metres, which discusses viable reinforcement learning techniques for atheletic skill learning by taking the 100-metre as an example.

I am looking for a PhD position of reinforcement learning, character animation, or robotic control. Of course, I'm also open to any other interesting research direction.
Please feel free to contact me !
